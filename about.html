About

We are dedicated to helping develop students into professional engineers

Our Mission Statement

As a chapter of the national organization, the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) - Cal Poly Pomona student branch shares IEEE's core purpose to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

Our Purpose

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers - Cal Poly Pomona student branch exists for the betterment of California State Polytechnic University, Pomona and its students. With an emphasis on Electrical and Computer Engineering, we focus on the technical, professional, and individual development of all our student engineers and non-engineers alike. We share IEEE's vision of being universally recognized for the contribution of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions.

What We Provide

  • Industry tours to major companies, such as Boeing, Northrop Grumman, and Western Digital.
  • Hands-on technical workshops, including our most popular semesterly soldering tutorial.
  • Informational presentations by various companies that include Raytheon, Thales, and Panasonic Avionics Corporation.
  • Endlesss networking and professional communication opportunities with industry professionals and alumni working in the industry.
  • Fun, engaging, social activities for our members to expand professional and social networks.
  • Professional development opportunities through locally hosted conferences, such as SPAx (Student Professional Awareness Experience).
