It all begins here

Become a Member!

You will recieve access to multiple IEEE resources that include:

  • product discounts
  • magazine subscriptions
  • resume building tools
  • career resources
  • and many more!

To find a full list of membership benefits, please visit IEEE's Global Benefits Finder and under Browse Member Benefits in the By Career Phase drop down box, choose Undergraduate Students or Graduate Students and USA in the By Country drop down box. Then click Go.

Student memberships:

  • Full Membership: $32 - Must apply between August 16 and February 28.*
  • Half Membership: $16 - Must apply between March 1 and August 15.**

*Membership applications processed between August 16 and December 31 will be applied to the following year.
**Half memberships are only valid until December 31 of the current year of application.

How to Join

To join, click the JOIN IEEE button below, proceed to Join as a student , and follow the instructions provided to complete your international membership application.

Almost There!

To complete your membership and become an official member of our student chapter, you must show your membership card to our secretary.

Please follow the step-by-step instructions below to ensure that you recieve our student branch benefits that include: free food at our meetings and events, priority to limited-access events, such as Industry Lunch n' Learns, a student branch t-shirt, and much more!

  1. Go to www.ieee.org and click Sign In in the top right corner of the page.
  2. Sign in using the email address and password associated with your IEEE account.
  3. Hover over your name in the top right corner of the page and click My Account.
  4. From the account homepage, view the Profile Settings menu on the left and click Membership and Subscription History under "Personalize your IEEE experience".
  5. Click Print your membership card, and your official electronic membership card will show as a printable document.
  6. Ensure your membership is valid so that we can confirm your membership is active.
  7. Save your membership card with the following naming format Lastname_Firstname_IEEE_2019-2020_MembershipCard.PDF.
  8. Email your membership card to our secretary at secretary.ieeecpp@gmail.com with the subject IEEE - 2019-2020 Membership Card - Firstname Lastname and wait for the confimation email to be sent out.
  9. Congratulations! You are now an official member of IEEE and student member of IEEE - Cal Poly Pomona
